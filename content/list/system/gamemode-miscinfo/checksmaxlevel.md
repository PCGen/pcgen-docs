## Status

New 5.4

## Syntax

`CHECKSMAXLEVEL:x`

## Parameters

-   x: Number (maximum level a characters base-saves
    may progress to).



What it does
------------

Specifies the maximum level a characters base-saves may progress to.

Example
-------

`CHECKSMAXLVL:20`

Sets the maximum level a characters base-saves may progress to at 20.
