## Status

New 5.4

## Syntax

`BABMAXLVL:x`

## Parameters

-   x: Number (maximum level a characters BAB may
    progress to).



What it does
------------

Specifies the maximum level a characters BAB may progress to.

Example
-------

`BABMAXLVL:20`

Limits a characters BAB progression to a maximum of 20.
