## Status

None

## Syntax

`BONUS:x`

## Parameters

-   x: Text (as per [global bonus
    tags](/list/global/bonus.html) ).



What it does
------------

Bonuses may be applied to stats as would be appropriate.

Example
-------

`BONUS:COMBAT|AC|1|TYPE=NaturalArmor`

Bonus of +1 to Natural Armor.

Where it is used
----------------

STATNAME Line.
