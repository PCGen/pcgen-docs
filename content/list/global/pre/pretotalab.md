## Status

New 6.03.x

## Syntax

`PRETOTALAB:x`

## Parameters

-   x: Number (Integer)



What it does
------------

This sets the total attack bonus requirement and considers both the base
attack bonu and the epic attack bonus.

Example
-------

`PRETOTALAB:21`

Required total attack bonus is 21.
