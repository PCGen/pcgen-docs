## Status

None

## Syntax

`PRESUBCLASS:x,y,y`

## Parameters

-   x: Number (number of needed occurrences).
-   y: Text (Subclass Name).



What it does
------------

Sets subclass requirements.

Examples
--------

`PRESUBCLASS:1,Evoker,Abjurer,Enchanter,Illusionist`

Character must be one of the listed subclasses.
