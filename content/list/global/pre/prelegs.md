## Status

None

## Syntax

`PRELEGSx:y`

## Parameters

-   x: EQ (Equals).
-   x: GT (Greater Than).
-   x: GTEQ (Greater Than or Equal to).
-   x: LT (Less Than).
-   x: LTEQ (Less Than or Equal to).
-   x: NEQ (Not Equal to).
-   y: Number (The number of legs to be compared to).



What it does
------------

Makes the amount of legs a character has a prerequisite.

Example
-------

`PRELEGSGTEQ:4`

Character must have at least 4 legs.
