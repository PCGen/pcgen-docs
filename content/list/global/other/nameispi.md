## Status

None

## Syntax

`NAMEISPI:x`

## Parameters

-   x: BOOLEAN (YES or NO)



What it does
------------

"YES" means it will bold the item in the program, to indicate that the
name of the item is the Product Identity. The Default is "NO".

Example
-------

`NAMEISPI:YES`

The name is the Product Identity
