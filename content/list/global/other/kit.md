## Status

New 5.10.4

## Syntax

`KIT:x|y|y`

## Parameters

-   x: Number (number of options user may select).
-   y: Text (Name of kit).



What it does
------------

Offers a choice of starting kits.

Examples
--------

`KIT:1|Wizard1|Illusionist1`

Offers a choice of the Wizard1 or Illusionist1 starting kits.
