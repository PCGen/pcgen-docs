## Status

None

## Syntax

`BONUS:STAT|BASESPELLSTAT|x`

## Parameters

-   x: Number, variable or formula (Number to adjust
    stat bonus by)



What it Does
------------

Adds to character's base spell stat bonus.

Example
-------

`BONUS:STAT|BASESPELLSTAT|1`

Adds 1 to all of the character's base spell stat bonuses.
