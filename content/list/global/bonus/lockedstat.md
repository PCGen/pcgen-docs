## Status

New 6.01.03

## Syntax

`BONUS:LOCKEDSTAT|x|y`

## Parameters

-   x: Text (Stat defined in loaded gameMode)
-   y: Number or formula



What it Does
------------

Used to bonus a locked ability score.

Example
-------

`BONUS:LOCKEDSTAT|INT|2`

Adds 2 to a locked "Intelligence" ability score.
