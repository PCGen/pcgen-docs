## Status

None

## Syntax

`HD:x`

## Parameters

-   x: Number (Hit Dice)



What it does
------------

Gives the companion bonus hit dice of its creature type.

Example
-------

`HD:2`

Defines the creature having two Hit Dice.

Where it is Used
----------------

FOLLOWER Line
