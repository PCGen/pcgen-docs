## Status

None

## Syntax

`GEAR:x`

## Parameters

-   x: Text (Item name)



What it does
------------

Grants the gear indicated to the character if they can afford to
purchase it.

Example
-------

`GEAR:Sack`

Would put a "Sack" into the PC's equipment if they can afford it.
