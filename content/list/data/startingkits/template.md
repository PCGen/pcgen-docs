## Status

New 5.9.2

## Syntax

`TEMPLATE:x|x`

## Parameters

-   x: Text (Template Name)



What it does
------------

This is a pipe (|) delimited list of templates that are granted by the
starting kit.

Example
-------

`TEMPLATE:Celestial`

Adds the "Celestial" template to the character.
