## Status

New 5.8

## Syntax

`FREE:x`

## Parameters

-   x: Property (YES or NO)



What it does
------------

Grants this item for free.

Example
-------

`FREE:YES`

The item that appears on this line will be granted to the character
without subtracting from the character's available pool.

Where it is used
----------------

FEAT or SKILL line
