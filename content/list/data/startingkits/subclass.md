## Status

New 5.9.3

## Syntax

`SUBCLASS:x`

## Parameters

-   x: Text (Subclass name)



What it does
------------

Sets a subclass choice. Will avoid the subclass chooser.

Example
-------

`SUBCLASS:Fast Hero`

Sets a subclass choice to "Fast Hero".
