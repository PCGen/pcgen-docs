## Status

New 5.9.3

## Syntax

`DEITY:x`

## Parameters

-   x: Text (Name of Deity)



What it does
------------

Sets character's Deity if qualified.

Example
-------

`DEITY:Kong`

Sets character's Deity to "Kong".
