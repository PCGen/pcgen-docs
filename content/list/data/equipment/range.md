## Status

None

## Syntax

`RANGE:x`

## Parameters

-   x: Number (Distance of one range increment)



What it does
------------

Sets the distance of the weapon's range increment.

Example
-------

`RANGE:50`

One range increment for this weapon is 50 ft.
