## Status

None

## Syntax

`CRITRANGE:x`

## Parameters

-   x: Number (Attack's critical chance)



What it does
------------

-   This is the weapon's critical threat range.
-   1 = 20, 2 = 19 & 20, 3 = 18 - 20, etc

Example
-------

`CRITRANGE:2`

Item criticals on roll of 19 or 20.
