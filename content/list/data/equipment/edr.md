## Status

None

## Syntax

`EDR:x`

## Parameters

-   x: Number (Integer greater than zero)



<span id="edr"></span> \*\*\* Updated 5.14.0

What it does
------------

Sets the Effective Damage Resistance of an item.

Example
-------

`EDR:5`

Item takes 5 points to destroy.
