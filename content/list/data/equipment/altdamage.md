## Status

None

## Syntax

`ALTDAMAGE:x`

## Parameters

-   x: Text (Secondary attack's damage)



What it does
------------

-   The secondary attack's damage.
-   This is used for double weapons only.

Example
-------

`ALTDAMAGE:1d6-1`

2nd head does 1d6-1 damage.
