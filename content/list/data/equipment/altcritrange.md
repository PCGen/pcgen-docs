## Status

None

## Syntax

`ALTCRITRANGE:x`

## Parameters

-   x: Number (Secondary attack's critical chance)



What it does
------------

-   The secondary attack's critical chance.
-   This is used for double weapons only.
-   1 = 20, 2 = 19 & 20, 3 = 18 - 20, etc

Example
-------

`ALTCRITRANGE:2`

2nd head criticals on roll of 19 or 20.
